Tryout Tips that get you Noticed!

May 8th, 2015 by Alek Mitrevski

Join us as we begin our next season. Tryouts will be held at Manzanita Park the last week of May and the first week of June. Visit the tryouts page for more information. Here are some tips to help you get noticed on the pitch during tryout week.

 

Get There Early and Warm Up

Arrive early to prepare for the up coming practice. This will give you enough time to do some pre workout warm-ups as you wait for the training to start. Use this time to get to know the other players in your age group.

 

Don't Be Shy

Introduce yourself to the coaches and let them know you're ready to play. Don't be shy, coaches are looking for that player that will stand out and take the lead.

 

Do NOT shoot on goal

It's very tempting to go out there when you first arrive and start blasting away at the goal prior to warming up. Simply do not do this, instead, find out where the gathering area is for the players in your age group and wait for further instruction.

 

Hustle!

Give it 110% all of the time everytime. Be consistent through the tryout period. When the coach calls you over, make it an effort to be the first to the coach.

 

Pay Attention/Be Attentive

Listen to the coaches instructions and be ready to act on his/her requests. When the coaches call you in, be the first to the coach. Pick up your speed and directly go to the coach. This shows your eagerness to be on the squad and will really make you stand out from other players.

 

Have Fun!

Coach Alek
