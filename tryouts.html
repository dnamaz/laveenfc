Laveen FC Tryouts

The 2014-2015 season has come to an end. It's time to prepare for the next season. Tryouts for the 2015-2016 teams will begin May 27th, 2015. Players will be put on a team based on age, skill and ability.

Who: U13 and U14 Boys and Girls

What: Laveen FC tryouts for 2015-2016 competitive season

When: May 27th-28th 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM, May 30th 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Where: Manzanita Park East Field

Checkin main tent at the field when you arrive. Arrive early to prepare for the training session. Bring plenty of water, soccer ball, cleats and shin guards.
