Laveen FC Tryouts

The 2015-2016 season has come to an end. It's time to prepare for the next season. Tryouts for the 2016-2017 teams will begin Monday May 16th, 2016. Players will be put on a team based on age, skill and ability.

New Age Regulation for 2016-2017

For the 2016-2017 season, a new age rule has been adopted by the US Youth Soccer Association. New and existing players will be placed on a team by their birth year. Please refer to the chart for your age bracket.
