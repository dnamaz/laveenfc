Coaches Corner - Profiles and Teams

 

Coach Alek

I have been involved with soccer most of my life. I started playing soccer in an organized league at the age of 10. It's been over 30 years of soccer experience at different levels of competition.

Currently, I play soccer in Tempe on Saturdays for the over 35 mens.

I started coaching a few years ago in the Laveen Soccer league. Since then, I've been working towards my USSF national license. I currently hold an 'E' license and working towards my 'D'.

My long term goals are to continue with this club and grow new teams each year and to continue with my coaching education.
