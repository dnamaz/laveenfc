How long is the season?

The club season runs from August 1st, 2015 to June 1st, 2016. We will be part of the Port of Subs Open league in Arizona. Season 1 starts 3rd week in September and season 2 starts the 3rd week in December. There will be a total of 12 games played across both seasons, 6 away and 6 home games. This is a competitive team and we will be playing teams from other cities in Arizona. Be prepared to travel when required. Each team will strive to play atleast 20 games which include league play, tournament play and scheduled friendlys.

How much does this cost?

The cost of the season is based on the number of players per team. This year, the club fee will be between 175 and 200 dollars for the season.

What do you get with your club fee?

Each player will receive a home and away jersey, shorts, socks and 1 training shirt. Bags and cold wear are extra.

How many practices are there per week?

We will hold at least 2 practice sessions per week with games on Saturday. On the off weeks where there are no games scheduled, a practice session or friendly can be setup, per the coaches schedule and avialability.

Are there tournaments?

Yes, each team registered with Laveen FC will participate in at least 2 tournaments per year.

Are the tournament fees included in the club fee?

No, tournament fees are in addition to the regular club fee.

How much does the tournament cost?

The cost per player will be determined based on the number of players attending the tournament and the tournament fee. Tournament fees will vary from tournament to tournament but roughly fall in the 500 dollar range.

What are the team age groups?

Laveen FC currently has openings for U13 and U14 teams. These are '02' and '03' boys and girls. At this time, we do not have older than U14 and younger than U13. Laveen FC will try to grow 2 new teams each year.

Are there monthly fees?

No, once you pay your club fee, there are no additional monthly fees. Tournaments will be on a case by case basis.

Do you offer montly payment options?

No, there are currently no payment option plans available.

Coach Alek
