Welcome to Laveen FC

Established in the summer of 2014, our mission, to provide a low cost alternative to club level play, to improve the skills and abilities of our youth soccer players and to provide a safe and friendly atmosphere to train.

The club started as a necessity for the parents of Laveen to extend their sons or daughters involvement with soccer in our local community and to provide our players a place they could train with licensed coaches.

We are a young club, and we have a lot of growth ahead of us, but we are growing.
